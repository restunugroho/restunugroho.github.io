1. Placement Recommendation for Taxi Drivers in Indonesia

Problem Statement

A major taxi company in Indonesia faced significant challenges during demand surges, such as heavy rain or major public events. During these periods, the order completion rate dropped sharply, causing customers to wait longer due to the unavailability of taxis. This led to a decline in customer trust while many drivers were roaming without passengers, wasting both fuel and time.

Solution & Approach

  • Mapped and clustered pickup hotspots using historical transaction data, grouping them by their location density
  • Developed a demand forecasting model for each cluster by time of day and day of week to predict future passenger needs
  • Integrated the forecasts into an optimization system that matches potential standby locations for each driver

Technology Stack

Python and BigQuery for data processing; QGIS for spatial analysis; DBSCAN for clustering; CatBoost for prediction; Kubeflow for batch prediction; MILP for optimization
